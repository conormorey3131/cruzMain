About Cruz Ireland

Cruz Ireland is a community of car enthusiasts dedicated to organizing and promoting car meets across Ireland. From Cork to Dingle, Mondello to Dublin, we bring together passionate car owners and admirers to celebrate automotive culture.

Our events showcase a diverse range of vehicles, from JDM classics to European performance cars, creating an inclusive environment for all automotive enthusiasts.
